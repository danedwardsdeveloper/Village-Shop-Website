Welcome to our market & cafe.

Situated on the edge of Sherwood Forest, Greenbriar Market & Cafe is the perfect starting point for your adventure. Stock up on fresh groceries for your forest explorations, savor the aroma of our freshly brewed artisan coffee, and indulge in our handcrafted pastries. Let the magic of Sherwood Forest inspire your taste buds as you embark on your journey.

Location

Greenbriar Market & Cafe
Acorn Lane, Sherwood Forest
NG25 0RQ

We have a car park for 6 cars, and there is additional parking on a lay-by on the road outside, plus unrestricted street parking

The store has its own dedicated bus stop & shelter - Greenbriar Market - served by the RH9 bus from Nottingham Victoria Bus Station to New Ollerton, Sherwood.

Nottingham Victoria Bus Station to Greenbriar Market & cafe ~ 30 minutes

New Ollerton, Sherwood to Greenbriar Market & cafe ~ 43 minutes

Get the Nottingham County bus app.

We have four bike racks at the side of the market, accessed through the outdoor seating area.

Why not stop to enjoy a freshly-made sandwich and a bean-to-cup coffee in our outdoor seating area?
